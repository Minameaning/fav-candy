Reese's History

Reese's candy was introduced in the year 1928 by Harry Burnett Reese.The original name for Reese's candy was “penny cups” due to their price at the time being only one cent in the early 1930s.

About the company The Hershey Company supplied H.B. Reese with the chocolate coating for his candy and maintained a close relationship with him throughout his candy ventures. In 1956, his six sons took over the business and eventually sold it to The Hershey Company in 1963.
